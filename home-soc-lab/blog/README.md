# Blog

Drafts and published writeups of detection engineering work, lab buildout, and lessons learned.

## Phase 1 deliverable

A "headline post" published at the end of Phase 1 (Week 13) walking through the home SOC lab buildout: architecture choices, what worked, what failed, key detections developed, and the AI triage agent. Target audience: hiring managers for SOC analyst and detection engineering roles.

## Structure

- `drafts/` — work-in-progress posts
- `published/` — posts that have been published externally (with a link back to where)
- `notes/` — research and outlines for future posts

## Style notes

- Write for someone curious but not in the field (avoid jargon, define when used)
- Show the actual KQL or Sigma — readers come for the technical content
- Always include the failure stories alongside successes
- Honest about scope: this is home lab work, not production SOC experience
- Cite sources for techniques and detections that adapt others' work
